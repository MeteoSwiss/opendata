## :white_check_mark: Hourly pollen measurements updated every 20 minutes

The hourly pollen measurements are now updated every 20 minutes, rather than once or twice a day. The data is available in the ´h_now´ file for each station ('Item') in the STAC collection [´ogd-pollen´](https://data.geo.admin.ch/browser/index.html#/collections/ch.meteoschweiz.ogd-pollen).

More about [this open data product](https://opendatadocs.meteoswiss.ch/a-data-groundbased/a7-pollen-stations).
