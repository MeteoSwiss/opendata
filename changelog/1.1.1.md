## :rocket: New data - announcement

- Pollen parameters are available as part of the [`Numerical weather forecasting model ICON-CH2-EPS`](https://opendatadocs.meteoswiss.ch/e-forecast-data/e2-e3-numerical-weather-forecasting-model#pollen-data) dataset since 21 May 2026
- [`Numerical weather analysis KENDA-CH1`](https://opendatadocs.meteoswiss.ch/e-forecast-data/e5-numerical-weather-analysis-data) dataset is live since 21 May 2026

MeteoSwiss blog post on these newly available open datasets: in [German](https://www.meteoschweiz.admin.ch/ueber-uns/meteoschweiz-blog/de/2026/05/pollenvorhersagen-analysedaten-aus-dem-wettermodell-icon-als-open-data-verfuegbar.html) , [French](https://www.meteosuisse.admin.ch/portrait/meteosuisse-blog/fr/2026/05/prevision-pollen-model-icon-libre-access.html) and [Italian](https://www.meteosvizzera.admin.ch/chi-siamo/meteosvizzera-blog/it/2026/05/previsione-pollini-e-dati-icon-come-open-data.html).
