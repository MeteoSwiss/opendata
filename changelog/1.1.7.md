## :recycle: New Open Data landing page

The new [Open Data landing page](https://www.meteoswiss.admin.ch/services-and-publications/service/open-data.html) makes all datasets more easily accessible via a filterable list. 
- Datasets that are not yet available but are planned are marked with the label `Announced`. The planned quarter or half-year is specified in the relevant documentation; see, for example, the [radiosoundings](https://www.meteoswiss.admin.ch/services-and-publications/service/open-data.html#ogd-q=radio&ogd-cat=&ogd-fmt=&ogd-kw=)
- Datasets that are available but not yet definitive can be marked with the label `Experimental`; currently we do not have any such cases.
