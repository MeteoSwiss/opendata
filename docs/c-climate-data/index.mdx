# Climate data

| No.    | Dataset/Collection | Data availability |
|:------:|:---------|:---------|
| **C1** | [**Climate stations - Homogeneous data series**](/c-climate-data/c1-climate-stations_homogeneous) <br></br> Temperature, precipitation, wind, sunshine, humidity, radiation and pressure <br></br> Daily, monthly and yearly | :white_check_mark: [**Data download**](/c-climate-data/c1-climate-stations_homogeneous#data-download) |
| **C2** | [**Climate precipitation stations – Homogeneous data series**](/c-climate-data/c2-climate-percipitation-stations_homogeneous) <br></br> Precipitation <br></br> Monthly and yearly | :white_check_mark: [**Data download**](/c-climate-data/c2-climate-percipitation-stations_homogeneous#data-download) |
| C3 | [Spatial climate analyses – Precipitation, temperature, sunshine](/c-climate-data/c3-ground-based-climate-data) <br></br> Daily, monthly and yearly | :calendar: expected to be available in the first quarter of 2026 |
| C4 | [Spatial climate analyses – Radiation, clouds](/c-climate-data/c4-satellite-based-climate-data) <br></br> Global, diffuse and direct radiation; cloud fractional cover <br></br> Daily, monthly and yearly | :calendar: expected to be available in the first quarter of 2026 |
| C5 | [Spatial climate analyses – Hail](/c-climate-data/c5-radar-based-climate-data) <br></br> Maximum hailstone size and number of hail days (total, with hail >2cm and with hail >4cm) <br></br> Monthly and yearly | :calendar: expected to be available in the first quarter of 2026 |
| **C6** | [**Climate normals**](/c-climate-data/c6-climate-normals) <br></br> Temperature, precipitation and sunshine for normal periods 1961-1990 and 1991-2020 <br></br> Monthly and yearly | :white_check_mark: [**Data download**](https://opendata.swiss/dataset/klimanormwerte) <br></br> via opendata.swiss, as ZIP (in TXT format) |
| **C7** | [**Spatial climate normals**](c-climate-data/c7-spatial-climate-normals) <br></br> Temperature, precipitation and sunshine with 2 km grid size for normal periods 1961-1990 and 1991-2020 <br></br> Monthly and yearly | :white_check_mark: [**Data download or Service**](https://opendata.swiss/de/dataset?q=klimanormwerte&sort=score%20desc%2C%20metadata_modified%20desc&organization=bundesamt-fur-meteorologie-und-klimatologie-meteoschweiz&res_format=SERVICE) <br></br> via opendata.swiss, in NetCDF format or as WMS and WMTS |
| **C8** | [**Climate scenarios CH2025 - DAILY-LOCAL**](/c-climate-data/c8-c9-climate-scenarios) <br></br> 30-year daily time series at the local scale for several variables at individual Swiss stations | :white_check_mark: [**Data download**](/c-climate-data/c8-c9-climate-scenarios#data-download) |
| **C9** | [**Climate scenarios CH2025 - DAILY-GRIDDED**](/c-climate-data/c8-c9-climate-scenarios) <br></br> 30-year daily time series at the local scale for several variables on a regular 1 km grid covering the area of Switzerland | :white_check_mark: [**Data download**](/c-climate-data/c8-c9-climate-scenarios#data-download) |
