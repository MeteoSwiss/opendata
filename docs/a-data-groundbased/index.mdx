# Ground-based measurements

| No.    | Dataset/Collection |
|:------:|:---------|
| **A1** | [**Automatic weather stations**](/a-data-groundbased/a1-automatic-weather-stations) <br></br> Temperature, precipitation, wind, sunshine, humidity, radiation and pressure <br></br> Every 10 minutes, hourly, daily, monthly and yearly |
| **A2** | [**Automatic precipitation stations**](/a-data-groundbased/a2-automatic-precipitation-stations) <br></br> Precipitation <br></br> Every 10 minutes, hourly, daily, monthly and yearly |
| **A3** | [**Automatic tower stations**](/a-data-groundbased/a3-automatic-tower-stations) <br></br> Temperature, wind, sunshine, humidity and radiation <br></br> Every 10 minutes, hourly, daily, monthly and yearly|
| **A4** | [**Automatic soil stations**](/a-data-groundbased/a4-automatic-soil-stations) <br></br> *Data not yet available* |
| **A5** | [**Manual precipitation stations**](/a-data-groundbased/a5-manual-precipitation-stations) <br></br> Precipitation and snow <br></br> Daily, monthly and yearly |
| **A6** | [**Totaliser precipitation stations – Measured values**](/a-data-groundbased/a6-totaliser-precipitation-stations) <br></br> Precipitation <br></br> Yearly |
| **A7** | [Pollen stations – Measured values](/a-data-groundbased/a7-pollen-stations) <br></br> Pollen concentration <br></br> Hourly and daily |
| **A8** | [Meteorological visual observations](/a-data-groundbased/a8-meteorological-visual-observations) <br></br> Visibility, current and past weather, ground conditions and clouds <br></br> Between two and eight times per day, monthly and yearly |
| **A9** | [Phenological observations](/a-data-groundbased/a9-phenological-observations) <br></br> Phenophases of 26 plant species <br></br> Yearly |
